---
question: Can I add my own content?
category: usage
order: 220
---

Yes. From the **Add Documents** page you can upload PDFs, text files, HTML, or Markdown, or paste a URL and ARCADIA will crawl and fetch it. Documents are auto-categorized into a knowledge domain, approved by you, then indexed alongside the built-in library. Once indexed, the AI references them when answering questions — just like any other document in the library.

ARCADIA automatically reindexes every night at 3:30 am so new content is available to the AI by morning. You can also trigger a manual reindex immediately from the Add Documents page.
