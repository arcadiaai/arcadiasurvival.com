## What you have

- An ARCADIA appliance — the small AI unit
- Power cord
- Optional USB speaker/microphone puck (for hands-free voice)
- This Quickstart and the full Manual

You do **not** need internet. ARCADIA works fully offline.

## 1. Power on

1. Place the unit on a flat, ventilated surface.
2. Plug in the power cord.
3. Press the power button on the back.
4. Wait about three minutes. The unit is silent — no screen, no keyboard needed.

ARCADIA is running when the WiFi network **ARCADIA** appears on your phone.

## 2. Connect

ARCADIA creates its own private WiFi. Any device can join:

- **Network:** `ARCADIA`
- **Password:** `survival`

Open any browser and type any address — `arcadia`, `start`, anything — and press Go. Every address routes to the ARCADIA home page.

The first time, accept the browser's certificate warning (Advanced → Proceed). One-time per device.

## 3. Ask your first question

On the home page, type a question into **Ask AI** — for example: *"How do I purify water without bleach?"* — and hit Send. ARCADIA answers using the local language model and cites sources from the on-device library.

## 4. Try the other panels

- **Search** — full-text search across the entire library
- **Browse** — documents organized by domain (medical, agriculture, etc.)
- **Maps** — pan and search the planet, offline
- **Voice** — tap the mic button, speak, hear the answer
