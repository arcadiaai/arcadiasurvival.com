We're building ARCADIA: a self-contained survival knowledge appliance that runs entirely offline.

The internet is the largest reference library humans have ever assembled — and the most fragile. Hurricanes, blackouts, censorship regimes, and ordinary outages all interrupt it. ARCADIA is a small, fanless box you keep on your kitchen counter that contains a curated subset of that library, plus a private AI to talk to it, and works whether the internet exists or not.

We'll write more here as we get closer to ship — build notes, field reports, and the occasional post on what's actually happening in the world that motivates this work.
